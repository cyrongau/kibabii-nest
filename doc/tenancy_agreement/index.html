4 SECURITY DEPOSIT

4.1 A refundable security deposit equivalent to one (1) month's rent shall be paid by the Tenant upon signing this Agreement.

4.2 The deposit shall be held in a designated Kibabii Nest Trust Account and shall not be utilized as rent for any month unless expressly agreed in writing.

4.3 The deposit shall be refunded within thirty (30) days of lease termination, less any deductions for:

  • Unpaid rent or utility bills
  • Cost of repairing damages beyond normal wear and tear
  • Replacement of lost or damaged inventory items
  • Cleaning fees if the room is left in unacceptable condition

4.4 An itemized deduction statement will be provided to the Tenant within the refund period.

5 RULES OF RESIDENCY

5.1 — Quiet Hours & Conduct

Quiet hours are observed from 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM daily. During examination periods, extended quiet hours apply 24/7. Excessive noise, disruptive behavior, or any activity that disturbs fellow residents is strictly prohibited.

5.2 — Visitors & Guests

Visitors are permitted between 8:00 AM and 9:00 PM only. All visitors must register at the reception and present valid identification. Overnight guests are not permitted without prior written approval from management. The Tenant is fully responsible for their visitors' conduct.

5.3 — Alcohol, Substances & Smoking

The possession, consumption, or distribution of alcohol, illegal drugs, or any controlled substances within the premises is strictly prohibited. Smoking and vaping are not permitted inside any building. Designated smoking areas are clearly marked outside.

5.4 — Cleanliness & Hygiene

Tenants must maintain their rooms and shared spaces in a clean and sanitary condition. Weekly inspections may be conducted by management. Garbage must be disposed of in designated bins only. Cooking inside rooms is prohibited unless the room is designated as self-contained with a kitchenette.

5.5 — Security & Access

Main gate access is from 6:00 AM to 10:30 PM. Tenants must carry their Kibabii Nest ID card at all times. Loss of keys/access cards must be reported immediately and a replacement fee of KES 800 applies.

5.6 — Prohibited Items

The following are strictly prohibited: electric heaters, hot plates, gas cylinders, candles, incense, weapons of any kind, pets, and any item deemed a fire or safety hazard by management.

7 TENANT OBLIGATIONS

  • Pay rent and all applicable charges on or before the due date.
  • Use the premises exclusively for residential student accommodation purposes.
  • Keep the room, furnishings, and shared areas clean and in good condition.
  • Report any damage, malfunction, or safety concern immediately via the Kibabii Nest platform.
  • Comply with all Rules of Residency (Section 5) and any additional house rules communicated by management.
  • Not sublet, assign, or share the room with any other person without written consent.
  • Allow management reasonable access for inspections and repairs (with 24-hour notice except in emergencies).
  • Not alter, paint, or make structural changes to the room.
  • Return all keys, access cards, and inventory items at the end of the tenancy.
  • Provide updated contact and next-of-kin information throughout the tenancy period.

8 LANDLORD / MANAGEMENT OBLIGATIONS

  • Ensure the property is safe, habitable, and well-maintained at all times.
  • Provide secure locking mechanisms, adequate lighting, and functional fire safety equipment.
  • Respond to maintenance requests within the stipulated timelines (Section 6).
  • Provide clean water supply, functional electrical systems, and internet connectivity (where included).
  • Respect tenant privacy and provide 24-hour written notice before inspections (except emergencies).
  • Maintain accurate records of all payments and provide receipts upon request.
  • Refund the security deposit within 30 days as per Section 4.
  • Not increase rent during the fixed lease period without mutual written agreement.
  • Provide a safe and discrimination-free environment for all tenants.

9 TERMINATION & BREACH

9.1 Either party may terminate this agreement with thirty (30) days' written notice.

9.2 The Landlord may terminate immediately upon: non-payment beyond 30 days, serious breach of residency rules, illegal activity, or behavior endangering others.

9.3 Early termination by the Tenant before the lease end date incurs a penalty equivalent to one month's rent.
